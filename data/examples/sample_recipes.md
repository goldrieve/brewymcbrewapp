# Example Recipes

This directory contains example beer recipes to help you get started.

## Classic American IPA

A hop-forward ale with citrus and pine notes.

**Grain Bill:**
- 4.5 kg 2-Row Pale Malt
- 450 g Crystal 40L
- 225 g Crystal 60L

**Hop Schedule:**
- 28 g Columbus (60 min)
- 28 g Centennial (15 min)
- 28 g Cascade (5 min)
- 56 g Citra (dry hop)

**Yeast:** US-05 (American Ale)

**Batch Size:** 19 liters

**Target Stats:**
- OG: 1.065
- FG: 1.012
- ABV: 7.0%
- IBU: 65
- SRM: 8

## Irish Stout

A smooth, roasty stout with coffee and chocolate notes.

**Grain Bill:**
- 3.6 kg 2-Row Pale Malt
- 450 g Roasted Barley
- 340 g Flaked Oats
- 225 g Chocolate Malt

**Hop Schedule:**
- 28 g East Kent Goldings (60 min)

**Yeast:** WLP004 (Irish Ale)

**Batch Size:** 19 liters

**Target Stats:**
- OG: 1.048
- FG: 1.012
- ABV: 4.7%
- IBU: 35
- SRM: 35

## Wheat Beer

A refreshing wheat beer perfect for summer.

**Grain Bill:**
- 2.3 kg Wheat Malt
- 1.8 kg Pilsner Malt

**Hop Schedule:**
- 21 g Hallertau (60 min)
- 14 g Saaz (15 min)

**Yeast:** WB-06 (Wheat Beer)

**Batch Size:** 19 liters

**Target Stats:**
- OG: 1.050
- FG: 1.010
- ABV: 5.2%
- IBU: 15
- SRM: 3
